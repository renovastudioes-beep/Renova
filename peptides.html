Questions? Answers.

Are these peptides for laboratory research only?

Yes. All ONYX Peptides products are sold strictly for in-vitro laboratory research purposes. They are not intended for consumption, diagnosis, treatment, or prevention of any disease.

How should lyophilized peptides be stored?

Store unopened vials at −20°C for long-term stability per standard laboratory protocols. Once reconstituted, follow institutional guidelines for short-term storage. Avoid repeated freeze-thaw cycles.

Do you provide Certificates of Analysis?

Yes. Every order includes a batch-specific COA with HPLC purity data, mass spectrometry identity confirmation, and endotoxin testing results.

What is your shipping policy?

We ship to qualified researchers and licensed research institutions within the United States via cold-chain packaging. Standard cold-chain shipping is $9.95; orders over $150 ship free. International shipping is available to verified institutional accounts only.
