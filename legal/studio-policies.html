Onyx Studios — Client policies

Last updated: July 3, 2026

These policies apply to salon, barber, extension, and clinical services at Onyx Studios. By completing intake forms and signing consent, you acknowledge that you have had the opportunity to review the sections below.

Cancellation & reschedule

  • Reschedules more than 48 hours before your appointment are free through the client portal or front desk.
  • Reschedules within 48 hours incur a $50 fee.
  • Same-day cancellations and no-shows may forfeit scheduling deposits or prepaid visit credits per your program agreement.
  • Late arrivals may shorten service time to protect the schedule for other clients.

Treatment consent & service agreement

  • Services, realistic outcomes, risks, and aftercare are explained before treatment begins.
  • Results vary by hair type, density, lifestyle, and adherence to home care.
  • Additional visits or adjustments may be recommended to achieve your goals.
  • You agree to follow pre- and post-visit instructions provided by your provider.

Color & chemical service consent

  • Color, bleach, highlights, balayage, toner, grey blending, keratin, and smoothing services require a separate consent reviewed with your provider.
  • Results depend on hair history, porosity, underlying pigment, and prior chemical services — outcomes cannot be guaranteed.
  • You must disclose all color, bleach, keratin, relaxer, or smoothing services within the last 12 months.
  • Patch or strand tests may be required. Your provider will explain whether a test is needed, completed, or waived for your service.
  • Adverse reactions may require stopping the service. Seek medical care if you experience severe irritation, swelling, or difficulty breathing.
  • You agree to follow processing, rinsing, and aftercare instructions provided at your visit.

Privacy, contact & data use

  • Contact details and visit notes are stored securely and are not sold to third parties.
  • We use your information to deliver services, schedule visits, and — with your permission — send relevant studio updates.
  • You may request access to or correction of your records at any time by contacting the studio.
  • Records are retained per studio policy and applicable law. See also our general privacy policy.

Photography & image release

  • Progress photography may be used internally for your chart and treatment planning.
  • Marketing, website, or social media use requires your separate opt-in on the photo release form.
  • You may request anonymity, cropping, or restrictions at any time.

Questions

Contact hello@onyxstudios.com or speak with your provider at your visit.
