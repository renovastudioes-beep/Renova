Damaged or Incorrect Orders

Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos. We will replace damaged cold-chain shipments or incorrect items at no charge.

Quality Concerns

If COA results do not match your batch documentation, contact support with your batch number. We will investigate and replace or refund at our discretion.

Non-Returnable Items

Due to the nature of research chemicals and cold-chain requirements, opened or reconstituted products cannot be returned. Unopened products may be eligible for return within 14 days if cold-chain integrity is verifiable.

Refunds

Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to the original payment method.
