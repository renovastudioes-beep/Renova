Questions? Answers.

Are these peptides for human consumption?

No. All RENVOA CLINIC products are sold strictly for in-vitro laboratory research purposes. They are not intended for human or animal consumption, diagnosis, treatment, or prevention of any disease.

How should I store lyophilized peptides?

Store unopened vials at −20°C for long-term stability. Once reconstituted, store at 2–8°C and use within 30 days. Avoid repeated freeze-thaw cycles.

Do you provide Certificates of Analysis?

Yes. Every order includes a batch-specific COA with HPLC purity data, mass spectrometry identity confirmation, and endotoxin testing results.
